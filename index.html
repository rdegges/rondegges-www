Ron Degges President, DHM

Welcome

Hi, I'm Ron Degges, President of DHM (Disciple's Home Missions), a father of 5, and a community spokesperson and advocate living in Indianapolis, Indiana.

For more than 30 years I have been actively involved in the ministries, serving in churches all over the US: Maryland, Iowa, Delaware, Virgina, and California.

My personal mission is to motivate and inspire individuals to help bring about a more peaceful and happy world where all individuals can live their lives to the fullest.

 

Through DHM, my team and I are working to transform the modern church into a place in which all people can feel safe and welcome, regardless of gender, race, or anything else.

If you'd like to contact me, the best way to get a hold of me is via email. My contact information is available below. I do my best to respond to all contact in a timely fashion.

With love,
- Ron
